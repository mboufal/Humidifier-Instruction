Instructions for Holmes Humidifier

DO's

Keep the cord out of heavy traffic areas
Keep away from children
Put it on an inside wall near an electrical outlet 4 inches (10cm) from the wall
Use only indoors, in designated household
Always place on a firm, flat, level surface
Place away from heat sources like stoves, radiators, and heaters

DON'TS

Don’t put the cord under the rugs or heat registers
Don’t place in a closed room, it might result in excessive humidity
Don’t place anything over the moisture outlet
Don’t drop or insert any objects into any opening
Don’t allow the moisture outlet to directly face the wall or any surface
Don’t place housing under the water or immerse in liquids
